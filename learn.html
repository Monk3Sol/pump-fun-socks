About Our Service

Pump Fun Socks is revolutionizing the way you get your favorite socks by combining digital currency with physical products. Our unique system allows you to purchase socks using $PUMP tokens and receive them directly at your doorstep.

How to Get Your Socks

1. Purchase $PUMP tokens through our website using the Buy button

2. Hold a minimum of 1000 $PUMP tokens in your wallet

3. Click the Redeem button when available to claim your physical socks

4. Fill out the shipping form with your delivery details

5. Receive your exclusive Pump Fun Socks within 2-4 weeks
